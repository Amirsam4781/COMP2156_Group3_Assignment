George Brown College (full name: The George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology)
is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario. There are three campuses in the downtown
area. One at St. James, another at Waterfont, and another at Casa Loma

UPDATE:

George Brown offers over a 100 full-time programs in design, business, ECE, engineering, 
health, and hospitality. The college offers diploma programs as well as advanced diplomas
and degrees.
